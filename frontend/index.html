How It Works

From RERA number to funding decision in three steps.

Enter Project Details

02

AI Agents Gather Data

What's Inside the Brief

Every report is a complete due-diligence package for your credit team.

RERA Verification

Automated CAPTCHA solving and data extraction directly from the MahaRERA government portal.

Financial Extraction

Gemini Vision reads CA Certificates and Form 3/5 PDFs to extract precise cost, revenue, and loan figures.

Eligibility Scoring

AI-generated 1-10 funding eligibility score with strengths, risks, and conditions for approval.

Clickable Documents

Market Validation
